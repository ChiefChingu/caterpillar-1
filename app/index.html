Find alerts near you: start here

Every year thousands of people report injuries caused by the oak processionary. This Spring promises to show an explosion of caterpillars due to the soft winter.

Never miss alerts in your neighborhood!

Know where to go and where to stay away! Together we can map where oaks are infected and where not. You can look for safe spots to let your children play, walk your dog, do your groceries, etc.

Add an alert by tapping on the map or using the + button. You can add infected trees and healthy trees.

If you alerted the community services you can also specify this in your map entry.

If you see an alert, but do not see the infection, you can flag that alert as possibly incorrect. If multiple people do this, the alert will be removed.
